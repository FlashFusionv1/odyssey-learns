# Grade 2 Lesson Outlines (50 Lessons)

## üìö READING & LANGUAGE ARTS (10 Lessons)

### Lesson 2-R-01: "Fluency Power: Reading with Expression"
**Duration:** 15-18 minutes  
**Complexity:** Intermediate  
**Learning Objectives:**
- Read with appropriate pacing and expression
- Use punctuation to guide intonation (periods, questions, exclamations)
- Practice repeated reading for fluency

**Key Activities:**
- Model reading video (fluent vs. choppy)
- Punctuation practice (read like you're excited/questioning)
- Echo reading (read after the narrator)
- Record yourself reading passage

**Assessment:** Read a 100-word passage aloud (fluency rubric: 70+ words per minute with accuracy)

---

### Lesson 2-R-02: "Asking Good Questions"
**Duration:** 15-18 minutes  
**Complexity:** Intermediate  
**Learning Objectives:**
- Generate questions before, during, and after reading
- Use question words (who, what, when, where, why, how)
- Understand questioning improves comprehension

**Key Activities:**
- "Question Detective" game (find answers in text)
- Before/during/after question chart
- Story: "The Curious Case of the Missing Cookie" (practice questioning)
- Create questions for a partner to answer

**Assessment:** Generate 3 questions about a passage, answer them correctly (5 comprehension questions)

---

### Lesson 2-R-03: "Making Predictions: What Happens Next?"
**Duration:** 15-18 minutes  
**Complexity:** Intermediate  
**Learning Objectives:**
- Use clues from text and pictures to predict
- Confirm or revise predictions while reading
- Understand predictions help engagement

**Key Activities:**
- "Predict the Story" interactive book (make predictions, then read to confirm)
- Picture walk prediction practice
- "What Will Happen Next?" cliffhanger stories
- Create story predictions chart

**Assessment:** Make 3 predictions about a story, explain reasoning (5 questions on prediction accuracy)

---

### Lesson 2-R-04: "Retelling Stories: Beginning, Middle, End"
**Duration:** 15-20 minutes  
**Complexity:** Intermediate  
**Learning Objectives:**
- Identify story structure (beginning, middle, end)
- Retell story in sequence with key details
- Use transition words (first, next, then, finally)

**Key Activities:**
- Story map graphic organizer
- Interactive retelling (drag events to correct order)
- "Story Scramble" game (arrange events in sequence)
- Record your own story retelling

**Assessment:** Retell a story including main characters, problem, and solution (5 sequencing questions)

---

### Lesson 2-R-05: "Author's Purpose: Why Did They Write It?"
**Duration:** 15-18 minutes  
**Complexity:** Intermediate to Advanced  
**Learning Objectives:**
- Identify author's purpose (to inform, persuade, entertain)
- Recognize text features that signal purpose
- Explain how you know the author's purpose

**Key Activities:**
- "Purpose Sort" game (categorize texts by purpose)
- Read three short texts, identify each purpose
- "Be the Author" activity (write with a specific purpose)
- Create a purpose poster

**Assessment:** Identify author's purpose in 5 different texts with evidence (5 questions, multiple choice)

---

### Lesson 2-R-06: "Punctuation Playground: Periods, Questions, Exclamations"
**Duration:** 15-18 minutes  
**Complexity:** Intermediate  
**Learning Objectives:**
- Use periods, question marks, and exclamation points correctly
- Understand how punctuation changes meaning
- Apply punctuation in writing

**Key Activities:**
- "Fix the Sentences" game (add correct punctuation)
- Punctuation performance (read sentences with correct tone)
- Sentence builder with punctuation choices
- Create comic strip with varied punctuation

**Assessment:** Add correct punctuation to 10 sentences (5 questions, choose correct mark)

---

### Lesson 2-R-07: "Compound Words: Two Words Become One"
**Duration:** 15-18 minutes  
**Complexity:** Intermediate

### Lesson 2-R-08: "Context Clues: Be a Word Detective"
**Duration:** 15-20 minutes  
**Complexity:** Intermediate to Advanced  
**Learning Objectives:**

- Apply context clues strategy

**Key Activities:**
- "Detective Training" video (how to find clues)

- "Clue Collector" game (underline helpful clues)
- Create sentences with context clues

### Lesson 2-R-09: "Main Idea and Details"
**Duration:** 15-20 minutes  
**Complexity:** Advanced

**Key Activities:**

- "Detail or Not?" sorting game
- Create main idea poster

**Assessment:** Identify main idea and 3 supporting details (5 questions, comprehension)

### Lesson 2-R-10: "Compare and Contrast"

## üìä ASSESSMENT PHILOSOPHY FOR GRADE 2

**Question Format:**
- Mix of multiple choice, short answer, and interactive tasks
- Visual aids with more text than Kindergarten
- Audio narration available but not mandatory
- 4-5 answer choices for complexity

**Adaptive Feedback:**
- Incorrect: "Let's review this concept together. Here's a hint..."
- Correct: "Excellent work! You're becoming an expert!"
- Mastery: "You've mastered this skill! Ready for the next challenge?"

**Scoring:**
- 85%+ = Full points + badge progress
- 70-84% = Partial points + targeted practice suggestions
- <70% = Retry with scaffolded instruction

**Retry Policy:**
- Unlimited attempts allowed
- Each retry presents new question variations
