## üìö READING FOUNDATIONS (10 Lessons)

### Lesson K-R-01: "Meet the ABCs: Letters A-E"
**Duration:** 10-12 minutes  
**Complexity:** Beginner  
**Learning Objectives:**
- Recognize uppercase and lowercase letters A, B, C, D, E
- Say the name of each letter
- Trace letters with finger/pencil

**Key Activities:**
- Animated letter parade (visual)
- Letter tracing practice
- "Find the Letter" scavenger hunt game
- Sing alphabet song (focus on A-E)

**Assessment:** Match uppercase to lowercase (5 questions, drag-and-drop)

---

**Duration:** 10-12 minutes  
**Complexity:** Beginner

## üìä ASSESSMENT PHILOSOPHY FOR KINDERGARTEN

**Question Format:** 
- Visual-heavy with minimal text
- Audio narration for all questions
- 3 answer choices maximum (4-5 for advanced)
- Drag-and-drop, tap, and click interactions

**Adaptive Feedback:**
- Incorrect: "Good try! Let's look at this again." (show hint)
- Correct: "Amazing work! You're a learning superstar!" (celebration animation)

**Scoring:**
- 80%+ = Full points + badge progress
- 60-79% = Partial points + encouragement
- <60% = Retry with scaffolded support

## üéØ IMPLEMENTATION PRIORITIES

**Immediate (Week 1):**
1. Life Skills lessons (emotional foundation critical)
2. Reading foundations (Lessons 1-5: all letters)
3. Math counting (Lessons 1-3: numbers 1-10)

**Week 2:**
4. Science (high engagement, visual learning)
5. Social Studies (community awareness)
6. Complete Reading and Math sequences

**Content Notes:**
- Maximum 15 minutes per lesson (attention span)
- Use animals, bright colors, and upbeat music
- Repetition is key (spiral review every 3 lessons)
- Parent involvement prompts in 50% of lessons
- Offline extension activities for each lesson
